What Do You Mean is a podcast created by Anna & Marla—two twenty-something sisters with nanoscopic attention spans. After laughing together for a quarter century, Marla said, "People need to hear our conversations." Anna said, "Okay."

The result? A rollercoaster with endless tracks & no braking system. Put on your beautiful listening helmets (safety, folks) & click HERE to listen to the very first episode!
